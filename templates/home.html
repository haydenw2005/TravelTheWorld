Tutorial

Welcome to Travel the World. The purpose of this website is to search and add countries to your wishlist, which will show up on your account's map. To add a country, click the search button on the left side of the navbar. Use the search bar to filter countries by letter. Click on a country and you will be directed to a page with facts and a button that gives the option to add it to your wishlist. To delete countries, click on the settings button on the right of the navbar then click "Delete Countries." Simply click the country's name to add it to the delete list, then when ready, press the trash button to delete all selected countries. Thanks for visiting!
